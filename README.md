# My-Totally-Awesome-Phone-Stand

Project Overview
-------------------------------------
Creating a phone stand in onshape CAD as a beginner, which hopefully retains the ability to hold a phone, and well at that. Started on July 12, 2026, 5 days before the July 17th final submission due date.


Intent/Reason Behind Project's Conception
-------------------------------------

Whenever I had to record videos for something, such as a project or a presentation, I had to either find or borrow a phone holder as I didn't have one capable of the perspectives I wanted to achieve. Because of things like the phone holder's shape, size, and range of motion, I kept having to rig the phone holder in variously precarious manners just to position the phone the way I wanted. As a result, due to the simplicity of a solution (or so I thought) and the time that was left to submit to this event, I decided to design a phone stand that could fit my purposes. I believe that it fits in with the theme of growth, or visible change as work is done, due to the fact that it can be modified to fit the users needs, and it is highly manueverable, making a wide variety of shots available along a number of axis, though only one axis can smoothly be moved across at a time due to the screw constraints.

Project Details
-------------------------------------

3D Model of Small Phone Holder, able to hold most sizes of phones

Pivots on a variety of axis

Moves across an extendable track
